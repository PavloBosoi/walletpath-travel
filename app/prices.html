Premium Subscriber Benefits

All The Best Deals

Free subscribers only get 1/3 of the deals I send out. Premium subscribers get all of them. On average, around 1-2 deals are sent out to the premium list for Europe each day. If there’s a great deal on a flight to your dream destination, I’ll make sure you know about it.

Choose Your Departure Airports

Only want cheap flights departing your home city? Or just a few of the closest airports? Premium members can choose which airports they receive notifications for (free subscribers receive alerts from entire regions) so you’ll only get deals you can use.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

I don’t want your hard earned cash if you don’t get any value from this. If, for any reason, you’re not happy with the premium subscription I’ll give you 100% of your money back in the first 30 days.

First To Know

Premium subscribers get the email alerts a full 30 minutes before the free subscribers. The really good deals sell out fairly quickly. The sooner you know, the better your chances are of getting in on the action.

No Ads

I run ads on the free list to cover the costs of emailing thousands of people. When you join the premium list you’ll never see an ad in the emails again.
