Press

Scott’s Cheap Flights is a freemium email list that sends out cheap airfare alerts to more than 750,000 subscribers worldwide.

Originally a hobby to let his friends know when great deals popped up, Scott’s Cheap Flights launched as an actual business in August 2015. Today there are 20 people on the team representing 9 countries; everyone works remotely and only a few of us have ever met face-to-face.

Scott’s Cheap Flights currently serves the United States, Canada, Latin America, the Caribbean, all of Europe, Asia, Australia, and New Zealand. (Africa coming soon!)

Scott and the team are available for interviews and can speak on a range of topics, including saving money on flights, frequent flyer miles, credit cards, entrepreneurship, and current travel news. Please email scott@scottscheapflights.com for press inquiries.
